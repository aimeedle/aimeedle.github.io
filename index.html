Clothing Sales & COVID-19

by Aimee Le

Mens, womens, and family clothing sales

How these retail stores are doing during the pandemic

Athleta

An apparel brand selling workout gear like sports bras and leggings for women reported net sales were down 8%, store sales were down 50%, with online sales up 49%

Source: Gap Inc.

Indochino

A Vancouver-based "made-to-measure" mens wear brand were forced to close 52 of its retail stores in the US and transfer business online, where it initially grew

Source: Drew Green, CEO of Indochino via WWD

Old Navy

A value-oriented apparel brand for families, net sales were down 42%, store sales were down 60%, with online sales up 20%. "Since the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic, Old Navy has seen an increase in its digital business."

Source: Gap Inc.
